About Moi

Seduced at a young age by the dark forces of skateboarding and hip hop culture, cursed from birth with a pesky sense of self-awareness and integrity, I've always known I was doomed to be... a creative. (dun dun dunnn)

After almost ten years of working in specialty coffee and making rap music on the side, I wanted something new so I started going on adventures: after-school program leader for a hip hop workshop at a local high school, music business intern in Los Angeles, and now, code school graduate and budding developer.

I fancy myself a front end/web developer and UX/UI designer who is passionate about combining tech with design, music and social change.

I still make music, you can find it here. I also build community through Artist's Way groups, music meetups and forward-thinking 12-step recovery groups.
